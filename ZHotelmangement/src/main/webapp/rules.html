Hostel Rules and Regulations

  • Maintain cleanliness in common areas and personal spaces.
  • Respect the privacy of fellow residents at all times.
  • Quiet hours are from 10 PM to 7 AM.
  • No loud music or disruptive behavior allowed.
  • Keep doors locked at all times for safety.
  • Guests are allowed only with prior permission.
  • Follow the designated smoking and drinking areas.
  • Report any maintenance issues to the management promptly.
  • Adhere to the meal timings set by the hostel.
  • Follow all fire safety regulations and procedures.
